Mockstock

MockStock is a web-app focused on giving our users a risk-free introduction to trading. Users are able to buy and sell stocks at their will with no real risk involved. Also, users can add friends and compete in weekly competitions to see who can gain the most "money" throughout a weeks worth of trading

Chess Move Optimizer

For my final project in Data Structures, myself and one of my classmates (Jett Crowson) created a C++ comand line program that calculated the most optimized path from one square to another on a chess board, for every piece. Users select their piece and choose two squares on the chess board. Using an implementation of Breadth First Traversal, our algorithm determines the shortest path between the starting and ending squares. Unfortunately, this code is not anywhere online. If you would like to see it, please email me and I will send it to you!
